Where can I get a good brie sammy? This place. I had been craving some brie for awhile and once again not wanting to leave my desk, I pulled up my food delivery app and came across Jax Bread Co when searching for brie. The Brie Melt stood out for me the most, with brie, bacon, arugula, and their housemade fruit compote on slices of french sourdough. The menu didn't specify a side but I didn't mind that. I was happy enough with just and sandwich.
